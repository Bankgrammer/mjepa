About Us

Who Are We

Established in 2013, Mjepatainment is a black youth run and controlled South African Company, it is also BEE compliant company in terms of HID determination. The company provides services to a wide range of clientele within the public, private sector.

Mjepatainment was found and owned by Jeffrey Jabulani Mazibuko (49% shareholder) and Precious Maleshoane Ramodike (51% shareholder) who are the only shareholders.

Jabulani who is driven by passion and new challenges, possesses an advanced certificate in Project Management, whilst Maleshoane is a very creative mindset individual who is able to diversify a lot of ideas possesses also an advanced certificate in Administration.

With a high level of technical experience and expertise working in different industries, the services offered are surely of high quality and guaranteed high efficiency.
